About Me

Hi, I'm Ethan Bayer. I'm a developer and independent musician interested in technology of all sorts as well as songwriting and music production. Check out my portfolio below!

Portfolio

Technology

Conditional Switch

Conditional Switch is a video game made using the Unity game engine as well as C# for scripting. In total it took me a few months to finish, and you can play it in your browser or download the game for Windows and MacOS.

Personal Website

I made this website, my personal portfolio, from scratch with HTML and CSS, which I learned over the course of a month thanks to the MDN Web Docs.

AutoCompressors

AutoCompressors is a Spigot plugin for the video game Minecraft, adding new functionality in the form of easily customizable items that, when in your inventory, can automatically convert specific items into other ones.

Ender-3

Over the course of a few years, I have heavily modified what originally was a Creality Ender-3 3D printer with both modifications developed by the incredible community around this printer as well as some of my own ideas and designs.

3D Design

So far, I have designed and published six simple and useful 3D models to solve problems I had. I used Fusion for the designs and 3D printed them on my Ender-3.

Calculator Pong

I made a simple clone of Pong and coded it in TI-Basic for the TI-84 Plus CE calculator.

Other

I also program in Python for small projects, and I have experience with Git (both through graphical tools and the command line), the Linux and MacOS shells, and the router software OpenWRT.

Music

Castle On The Hill

I covered Ed Sheeran's song "Castle on the Hill" and released it as part of Grey Warren's album The Songs We Leave Behind. I sang and played guitar for the song, as well as helping produce it.

Performance

I have played guitar for over nine years, and I have performed various times.
